TRADITIONAL MUSIC/FOLK

Tarpa Music

Tarpa is a traditional wind instrument made of bamboo, commonly used in tribal communities of Dadra and Nagar Haveli. It is played during festive occasions and celebrations.

Folk Songs

Various folk songs are sung to depict the local way of life, traditions, and legends. These songs often accompany local dance forms and are passed down through generations.

Ghumer Dance and Music

Ghumer is a folk dance form performed by the Warli tribe in Dadra and Nagar Haveli. It is accompanied by traditional music played on instruments like the Tarpa and Dholki.

Dholki and Dhol

These are percussion instruments used in traditional music and dance performances. The Dholki is a smaller drum, while the Dhol is a larger, double-headed drum.

Lezim Dance and Music

Lezim is a traditional folk dance that involves participants holding small musical instruments called lezims. These instruments produce rhythmic beats when shaken.

Gher Dance

This is a lively tribal dance form accompanied by traditional music. It involves intricate footwork and hand movements, often performed during festivals and celebrations.

Bhavai Music

Bhavai is a traditional folk theatre form that incorporates music, dance, and drama. The music used in Bhavai performances is an essential component of this art form.

Mand and Garba Music

While Garba and Dandiya Raas are more commonly associated with the neighboring state of Gujarat, they are also popular in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu, especially during Navratri and other festive occasions.
