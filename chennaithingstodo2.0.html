THINGS TO DO

VISIT MARINA BEACH:

It is one of the longest urban beaches in the world and a popular destination for both tourists and locals. You can take a leisurely stroll along the sandy shores.

EXPLORE HISTORIC SITES

Chennai is rich in history, and you can explore various historical and cultural landmarks. Some must-visit places include Fort St. George, etc

INCLUDE IN SOUTH INDIAN CUISINE

Chennai is renowned for its delicious South Indian cuisine. Be sure to try local specialties like dosa, idli, vada .

ATTEND A CLASSICAL MUSIC

Chennai is a cultural hub known for its classical music (Carnatic) and dance (Bharatanatyam) traditions. Check out the schedules of local concert halls
