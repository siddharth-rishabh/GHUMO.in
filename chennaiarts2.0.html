BHARATHANATYAM

It is one of the classical dance forms of India and is widely regarded as one of the most ancient and traditional dance styles.

Tanjore Painting

It is a traditional South Indian art form that originated in the town of Thanjavur in Tamil Nadu. Chennai is a hub for Tanjore painting artists and galleries.

kOLAM

Kolam, also known as Rangoli or Muggulu, is a traditional and decorative art form practiced in South India, particularly in the states of Tamil Nadu.

Contemporary Art

Chennai has a growing contemporary art scene showcasing the work of contemporary artists in various mediums, including painting, sculpture etc.
