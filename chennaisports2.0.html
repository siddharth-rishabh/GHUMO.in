SILAMBAM

Silambam, also spelled as "Silambattam" or "Silambattu," is a traditional Indian martial art that originated in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

JALLI KAATU

"Jalli Kattu," also known as "Jallikattu," is a traditional and controversial bull-taming sport that is primarily associated with the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

KABADDI
