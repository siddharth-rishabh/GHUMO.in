Navratri and Garba Festival

Navratri is a nine-night festival celebrated with great enthusiasm in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu. It is dedicated to the worship of the goddess Durga in her various forms. During this festival, people participate in Garba and Dandiya Raas, which are traditional folk dances.

Garba and Dandiya Raas are vibrant and energetic dance forms that involve rhythmic movements and the use of sticks (Dandiya) or claps (Garba). These dances are performed in colorful traditional attire and are a significant part of the Navratri celebrations.

Navratri is a time for devotion, music, dance, and social gatherings. It brings communities together to celebrate the triumph of good over evil and the blessings of the goddess Durga. The festival creates an atmosphere of joy and unity among the people of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu.
