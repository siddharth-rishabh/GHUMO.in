THINGS TO DO

Visit the Beaches

  • Enjoy the serene beaches of Daman and Diu such as Nagoa Beach, Jampore Beach, and Devka Beach.
  • Engage in water sports like parasailing, jet skiing, and banana boat rides.

Explore Forts and Historical Sites

  • Visit the historical forts like Diu Fort, Moti Daman Fort, and Nani Daman Fort to learn about the region's colonial history.
  • Explore the Church of St. Paul and Church of St. Francis of Assisi in Diu, showcasing Portuguese architecture.

Warli Art Workshops

Participate in or observe workshops on Warli painting, a traditional tribal art form, to learn about the local culture.

Bamboo and Cane Workshops

Attend workshops where you can see artisans create beautiful crafts from bamboo and cane. You might even get a chance to try your hand at it.

Enjoy Tribal Culture

Immerse yourself in the tribal culture of Dadra and Nagar Haveli by visiting tribal villages and experiencing their customs, rituals, and traditional dance forms.

Nature Walks and Bird Watching

Take a stroll through the lush greenery of Dadra and Nagar Haveli. Visit places like Vanganga Lake Garden to enjoy nature's beauty and observe various bird species.

Visit the Tadkeshwar Mahadev Temple

Explore this ancient Hindu temple in Dadra and Nagar Haveli known for its tranquil surroundings and cultural significance.

Attend Local Festivals

Plan your visit around local festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Ganesh Chaturthi to experience the vibrant celebrations and cultural traditions of the region.

Explore Tribal Museums

Visit museums like the Tribal Cultural Museum in Silvassa to learn about the rich cultural heritage of the tribal communities.

Visit the Vasona Lion Safari Park

Experience a safari in this park to see wildlife including lions, deer, and other animals native to the region.

Enjoy Water Parks and Resorts

Relax and have fun at water parks like Mirasol Water Park in Daman or resorts offering.
